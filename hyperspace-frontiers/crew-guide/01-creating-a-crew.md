To play Hyperspace Frontiers you assemble a crew of 5 characters. It is up to you whether you want to create an elite squad of well equipped warriors, a ragtag group of plucky adventurers, or maybe something entirely different. The skills and equipment in this book are only a starting point, with far more variety and options available in each of the Episodes.

## New Crews

To start creating a crew you’ll need a Crew Roster sheet (found at the end of this book), though it’s usually worth working out what you want on paper first before writing down your choices. You begin with 5 characters each with a Level of 1, and a starting Attack and Defence attribute of 3. You will then work through each character increasing their stats, giving them Skills, and purchasing weapons for them to use.

For a normal crew, you have 16 Levels to spend across all of your characters. Each time you increase the Level of a character you also choose to increase either the Attack or Defence attribute of that character. The only restriction to how this is done are:

- No character may have a Level that is more than double that of your lowest Level character.
- No character can ever have their Attack or Defence attributes more than double the other attribute.

If you wish to play a campaign that starts at a lower level, with more room to grow, the starting Levels should be set to 7. If you want to play a higher level crew, especially for one-off games with bigger and bolder skills, the starting Levels should be increased to 26.

## Character Skills

Once you have set the Level of each of your characters you can then choose their Skills. There are a range of Skills on the next few pages to choose from. Each Skill provides the character with abilities and bonuses that will determine how they fare on the battlefield.

Character can be given up to 4 Skills each, and may not take the same Skill twice. Skills also have an associated rank based on how powerful it is, and characters may select Skills up to a combined rank equal to their Level.

## Skill Sets and Alignment

Skills are organised into sets representing specific roles on the battlefield. You don’t have to choose from just one set for your Skills, but doing so provides a small bonus.

When all of a character’s Skills are from the same set they gain access to that set’s Alignment Skill. This is a free Skill that can’t be taken any other way, and the character keeps it so long as they do not have Skills from any other set. An Alignment Skill is shown with a rank of A instead of a number.

## Weapons and Equipment

Each of your characters must be equipped with weapons to use in their battles. All characters have access to the "Unarmed" weapon and you can purchase additional weapons using Credits. Not including the Unarmed weapon, characters may have up to 3 weapons.

Each character in a new crew has 25 Credits to spend on weapons. These Credits are not transferable to other characters. You also have an additional 30 Credits to spend on weapons which you can divide among your characters as you like.

Any Credits from this last portion that are left over can be kept and recorded on your crew roster. For one-off battles this is of no benefit but can be used in Campaigns.

## Weapon Traits

Traits, like the Skills your characters have, can significantly change how a weapon operates. They make your weapons hit harder, shoot faster, or knock your opponents clean off their feet. The rules for each Trait is listed below, with additional Traits found in Episode releases.

| Trait           | Rule                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Accurate        | You may re-roll any failed dice in the initial roll of an Attack roll.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| Armour Piercing | If this weapon would cause a character to become Grazed they instead become Injured. This does not count as the Grazed result on the damage table.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| Impact          | When this weapon targets a character, it also targets all other characters within their Combat Range. Make your attack roll once as normal, then check if it would cause damage to all targeted characters.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| Destructive     | Critical successes with this weapon allow you to roll 2 additional dice instead of 1.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Executioner     | If this weapon would cause a character to become Injured they instead retreat. This does not count as the Grazed result on the damage table.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| Heavy           | This weapon cannot be used if the character has declared a Move or Advance action this activation.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| Knockback       | An attack with this weapon causes the target to be moved backwards after damage has been resolved. They move a number of inches equal to the difference in the Attack roll. The direction they travel is determined by drawing a line from the centre of the attacking model towards the centre of the target model, the model moves along this line away from the attacker. If the character encounters any terrain, the edge of the battlefield, or other characters during this movement, the character stops moving. |
| Light           | This weapon does not require the character to perform a Focus action to be used At Range.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| Rapid Fire      | A character that makes an attack with this weapon against a target within 6", and has taken a Focus action, adds +2 to the Power of the weapon.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| Reach           | A character with this weapon may move up to 2" before making an Attack action. They may move into Combat Range of an enemy character but otherwise treat this as a normal Move action with the normal restrictions.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| Stun            | A character that would take damage from this weapon also loses 1 AP for a Grazed result, and 2 AP for an Injured result. However, an attack from this weapon can never cause a character to retreat.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
