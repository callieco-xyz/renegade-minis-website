## Welcome to the Frontier

Beyond the shining worlds of the Gilded Stars stretch the Hyperspace Frontiers. Vast regions of space far larger, and far stranger, than the 'civilised' Inner Systems. They say life on the frontier is free, sometimes they just mean cheap. But for those willing, and able, to do what it takes, there is opportunity and adventure in abundance.

No two regions of the frontiers are the same, each contains a bewildering array of worlds, cultures, and conflicts: from dust-choked mining colonies where fortunes are dug straight out of the ground, only to be snatched away by marauding pirates; fledgling alien empires, crumbling under the strain of rapid expansion, scrambling to retain their grip on systems they can no longer control; and quiet corners of the galaxy, where strange ruins from a forgotten age draw in prospectors willing to stop at nothing for the chance to find true power.

Without the astronomical engines that make travel so simple in the Gilded Stars, travel between worlds on the frontier is severely limited. Only small Jump Ships are capable of navigating untamed hyperspace, too small to carry much more than a handful of crew with very little space left over for cargo. Anything larger is prone to being torn apart by unpredictable dimensional shearing.

It is the crews that risk these journeys that carry news, trouble, and opportunity from one world to the next. Some crews play at being heroes, other really are, throwing their lot in with any downtrodden settlers that need defending. Others chase profit wherever it can be found, running cargo, hunting relics, or building their own power one job at a time.

## The Game

Hyperspace Frontiers is a miniatures skirmish game about legendary characters. Heroes, rogues, and ruthless figures who throw themselves into danger, or create it, as they leave their mark on the frontier.

This game is presented as an anthology series that explores these strange regions of space through releases called Episodes. Each new episode brings new worlds to discover, new rules for creating your characters, new ways to play, and lots more besides. 

This book provides the core rules needed to play a game of Hyperspace Frontiers. There is also a companion book to this one called  the "Hyperspace Frontiers: Crew Guide, which provides rules on creating your crew, how battles are organised, as well as rules for playing in a campaign.

## Designer’s Note

Hyperspace Frontiers is a story that has grown in the telling. From an early concept for some fun skirmish rules, into an idea that I can't get out of my head. On the table I think I have captured something that is fluid, cinematic at times, with a simple skill system that makes really iconic character moments. It's not a simulation, nor is it grim or dark, it's a Saturday morning cartoon with all the violence left in.

The idea of telling this story as an anthology has also become a really key part of the game. Moving away from pure science-fiction to create a stranger, more fantastical, universe that feels genuinely interesting to explore. These Episodes create opportunity for new stories, new rules, and new ideas, while leaving enough space for you to make something unique.

There's plenty more of this world to explore and I can't wait to see what you all make with it.
