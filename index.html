Welcome to Renegade Minis

Hi, I’m Callie, aka Renegade Minis. I’m a Scottish artist making miniatures and tabletop games, usually of the skirmish variety. Most of the miniatures I make are customisable, multi-part kits, and can be used in the games I create or in other systems. I do sometimes choose to make more unique characters, vehicles, monsters, and have been known to make things like terrain, bases, tokens and more.

If you're looking for something more custom for your collection, event, or game, I am available for commissions. Find out more about my commission work and how to get in touch.
