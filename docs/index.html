Momentum Wargame System

Momentum is the core system behind our games, offering a common ruleset that makes it easy to pick up new games. It features a simple yet dynamic D6 mechanic and a flexible activation system that keeps battles strategic and engaging. Each game built on Momentum adds unique mechanics, factions, and scenarios, but the fundamentals remain the same, learn one, and the rest come naturally.

Coming Soon! Hyperspace Frontier

Hyperspace Frontier is a dynamic tabletop skirmish game set in a lawless region of of space. You will be create your own small crew and lead them through deadly raids, high stakes shoot-outs, and tense last stands, building their legend with every battle.

The Broken Spur is a wide and wild frontier and you will find heroes, villains, and everything in-between there. The rules allow you to craft a unique crew that can evolve as they experience victory and defeat, gaining new skills and perks, as well as potentially suffering from injuries or other penalties. The miniatures for the game are designed to let you customise the look of your crew to your own ideas, mixing different parts and being compatible with many kits from other creators.

The game uses the Momentum System for it's core rules without modification so if you have already played a game in that system you will be able to begin playing Hyperspace Frontier as soon as you put together your first crew.

Commission Miniature Sculpting

Ang Gree, commissioned by the SquadChaos for their Thrudball Charity Blood Bowl Tournament, painted by Steven at Arms Length Studio

We take on a limited number of commissions: from individual collectors and event organisers to studios looking to outsource miniature design. Whether it’s a one-off sculpt or a full range, the goal is always the same: bring your vision to life while staying true to the kind of minis we believe in.

Everything we make is built with personality and made to be fun to paint. We focus on strong clean designs, expressive details, and a style that nods to the hand-sculpted classics while still feeling fresh. If you’re after something with character, something that stands out on the table, we’d love to hear from you.

About Renegade Minis

We are Renegade Minis, a two-person team based in Scotland creating original tabletop games and awesome digital miniatures with character. Inspired by the charm of old-school wargames: bold sculpts, chaotic lore, and rules that left plenty of room for imagination.

We’re combining that love of the old with fresh ideas, new mechanics, and a desire to make something that stands on its own. We build games that are fun to play, full of personality, and designed to spark creativity, whether you're here to collect minis, write your own stories, or throw yourself into a world of dice, rules, and mayhem.

This is what we love doing, and we’re just getting started.
