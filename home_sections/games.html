Momentum Wargame System

Momentum is the core system behind our games, offering a common ruleset that makes it easy to pick up new games. It features a simple yet dynamic D6 mechanic and a flexible activation system that keeps battles strategic and engaging. Each game built on Momentum adds unique mechanics, factions, and scenarios, but the fundamentals remain the same, learn one, and the rest come naturally.

Coming Soon! Hyperspace Frontier

Cover art by Lukasz Kowalczuk

Hyperspace Frontier is a dynamic tabletop skirmish game set in a lawless region of of space. You will be create your own small crew and lead them through deadly raids, high stakes shoot-outs, and tense last stands, building their legend with every battle.

The Broken Spur is a wide and wild frontier and you will find heroes, villains, and everything in-between there. The rules allow you to craft a unique crew that can evolve as they experience victory and defeat, gaining new skills and perks, as well as potentially suffering from injuries or other penalties. The miniatures for the game are designed to let you customise the look of your crew to your own ideas, mixing different parts and being compatible with many kits from other creators.

The game uses the Momentum System for it's core rules without modification so if you have already played a game in that system you will be able to begin playing Hyperspace Frontier as soon as you put together your first crew.
