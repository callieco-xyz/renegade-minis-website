Our first full game, Hyperspace Frontiers, is currently in development - scheduled for a 2026 release.

Hyperspace Frontiers is a sci-fi miniatures skirmish game focused on tight conflicts between small crews of 5 characters. The setting is explored as an anthology, moving from one frontier to the next, expanding the setting, the characters, and the ways to play as it goes. Check back soon, or follow us on our primary social media on Mastodon, or over on BlueSky or Instagram, to get information as it's released.
