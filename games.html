Hyperspace Frontiers is a sci-fi miniatures skirmish game currently in development, focused on tight conflicts between small crews of 5 characters. The setting is explored as an anthology, moving from one frontier to the next, expanding the setting, the characters, and the ways to play as it goes. Check back soon, or follow us on Mastodon, BlueSky, or Instagram, to get information as it's released.
